About Us.

Education is the backbone of a nation. With a view to strengthening that backbone throughout our whole country Prottoy has started its way. Every year for getting treated with quality education lots of students come to Dhaka living their friends and family behind. But still they face a lot of problems in their daily life. They struggle there to have healthy and nutritious food. They also face problems finding healthy environment and ultimately fall sick. And financial crisis is also there. Our mission is to wipe out all these problems by providing quality education in affordable range to all those students who have thirst for knowledge.
